RESONANCE · 004 · Hindustani Classical
Raga Yaman
The Grammar of Longing Without Collapse
P1 → S3 → F1
The Teevra Ma (sharpened fourth) creates structural reaching without grasping. P1 projection that moves toward an attractor (S3) and returns to coherence (F1) without T3. The contrast with Bhairavi is the corpus's strongest teaching moment: same tradition, same grammar, structurally opposite arcs.

RESONANCE · 005 · Hindustani Classical
Raga Darbari Kanada
T3 as Structural Home
T3 → T3 → T3
The gamak (oscillatory ornament) on Komal Ga, Komal Ni, Komal Re produces micro-T3 at the phrase level, sustaining T3 at the macro level for 45–90 minutes without exit. Vairagya rasa: settled recognition of impermanence. The raga does not exit T3 because T3 is the structural home, not a transient state. Direct parallel: Track v Infrastructure.
