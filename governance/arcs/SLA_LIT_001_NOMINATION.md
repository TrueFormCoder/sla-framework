The oracle's "multi-truth" is not positional disagreement — it is temporal concealment.

## Evidence for R1-A (Structurally Unavailable)

Three structural markers confirm R1-A rather than R1-B (missed window):

**1. The loop precedes the subject.** The L2 (fate pattern) was active before Oedipus was born.
R1 cannot initiate against a loop that predates the entity who would initiate it.

**2. Escape attempts are convergence accelerators.** In R1-B (missed window), there was
a window that closed. In Oedipus Rex, there was never a window — each escape attempt
is a F4 that closes the remaining distance to T3. This is the definitional R1-A signature.

**3. Recognition arrives at structural impossibility.** T1 (recognition fracture) fires
at the exact moment when acting on the knowledge would change nothing.
This is the "awareness arrives only when it can no longer change the outcome"
canon line — it describes this specimen precisely.

## P4 Evidence

P4 (Witness Function / Expansion Funnel) is present in three distinct forms:

**P4 as Chorus.** The Chorus observes, comments, and cannot alter. This is the
theatrical form of P4 — structural external witnessing of T3 that changes nothing
about the collapse but constitutes it as witnessed.

**P4 as audience across 2,400 years.** Every performance since 429 BCE has been
a P4 witness event — the collapse is re-witnessed without altering its structure.
The arc is preserved precisely because it witnesses without acting.

**P4 as Tiresias.** The blind prophet who sees but cannot be heard — he speaks
the truth that Oedipus cannot receive. This is P4 at the within-drama scale.
Tiresias is the P4 node inside the Circle structure of the play.

The persistence of P4 across three scales (dramatic, theatrical, historical)
without any R1 anywhere in the 2,400-year record is the strongest available
confirmation that the arc is structural rather than contingent.
